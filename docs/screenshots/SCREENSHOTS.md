# Screenshot Guide

Add the following screenshots to this folder before publishing.
The README references them by the exact filenames listed below.

---

## 1. `01-search-query.png`

**What to capture:** The initial state with a query typed into the search bar, before submitting.

**Steps:**
1. Open `http://localhost:5173`
2. Type a query such as:
   `I need a backend engineer who knows FastAPI and SQL`
3. Take a screenshot **before** clicking Search.

**Ideal size:** full browser window, or cropped to the search panel.

---

## 2. `02-skills-review.png`

**What to capture:** The skill chips panel that appears after Claude extracts skills from the query.

**Steps:**
1. Submit the query from step 1.
2. Wait for the skill chips to appear (e.g. `fastapi`, `sql`, `python`).
3. Optionally add or remove one chip to show the editing capability.
4. Take a screenshot showing the chips and the OK button.

---

## 3. `03-ranked-results.png`

**What to capture:** The results table showing AI-ranked employees with their reasons.

**Steps:**
1. Click OK on the skills review panel.
2. Wait for results to load.
3. Take a screenshot of the full results table, including the Reason column.

---

## Tips

- Use a light browser theme for better readability in the README.
- Window width ~1280 px gives a clean layout.
- Crop out browser chrome (address bar, tabs) to keep focus on the app.
- PNG format preferred for sharpness.
